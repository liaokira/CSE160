Assignment 1 Part 3

Triangle drawing program

Click anywhere on the canvas 3 times to create a triangle.
The triangle will be drawn in the color you selected.
The vertices of the triangles can be dragged to reposition them.
Delete the last triangle drawn by pressing the 'delete last triangle' button.
Dictionaries displaying the position and color of the triangles will appear at the bottom.
Also added a div to display the currently selected color.
Made part 2 pretty easy ^_^

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
